Our Heritage: From Shop Floor to Leadership

The visionary journey of M. Mansoor Rahiman

Stage 1: Shop-Floor Roots

Early Career

Our Managing Director, M. Mansoor Rahiman, began his career as a shop-floor helper in lathe operations, mastering the intricacies of machining from the ground up.

Stage 2: Entrepreneurial Leap

1990

Driven by a passion for precision, he founded the company with a modest setup of just two lathe machines, building it from a modest setup.

Stage 3: Formal Establishment

1994

AMR Industrials was formally established as a corporate entity, marking the beginning of our evolution into a trusted modern manufacturing organization.

Stage 4: Sustainable Growth

Present Day

Through decades of discipline and customer trust, we've evolved into a trusted manufacturing partner with 60+ specialized machines and 45-50 skilled professionals.
